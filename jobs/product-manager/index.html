Senior Product Manager / Product Manager

Back to all jobs

Want to work in the hottest game startup in Helsinki and ship multiple games a year? Is developing games your passion? Join Futureplay, we are looking for you!

As a Product Manager at Futureplay equipped with data, analytics, competitive insights and growth hacking experience your task is to create the next best, engaging, fun and innovative games while working with an experienced team of programmers, artists and game designers.

You’ll be responsible for complementing the game’s design with a data-driven mindset to help in designing, measuring and improving all aspects of the product’s core and meta game to monetization and economy balance. You excel at making sure the team is up to speed with the vision and findings based on data and experience that you clearly communicate through analytical, verbal, written or visual materials while driving the Futureplay mentality of game development from start to finish and especially when the game is live.

We require:
  • 5+ years of game product management experience
  • Hands-on product management in 3 shipped/live ops free-to-play games
  • Versatile skills to be on top of data, analytics and metrics systems
  • Deep understanding of the KPIs, mechanics and tricks of free-to-play
  • Understanding the math and balance with in-game economies
  • Excellent communication skills in English and and presentation magic
  • Great teamwork skills
We admire:
  • Scripting/programming/database skills
  • Experience from mobile ad monetization
  • Deep understanding of social systems in games
  • Love / hate relationship with UA
  • Keen eye and experience in behavioral economics
  • Having a beautiful mind
We offer:
  • True ownership and responsibility in one of the most creative gaming startups
  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Great working conditions in an exciting and agile environment
  • Flexible working hours
  • Space to grow and learn
  • Flat hierarchy with a “no bullshit” culture
  • Work in one of the most relevant game development capitals in Europe
How to apply:

If you’re the talent we are looking for, send your application and CV in English together with a relevant portfolio to careers@futureplay.fi with the subject “Senior Product Manager”.

About Futureplay

Futureplay, was founded in 2015 by five professionals to build the future standard for mobile games - and to create a great company where the best people want to work in. Our focus is on creating a new category of “View-to-Play” games – broadly accessible gameplay, with a natural integration and rewarding ad-based monetization. We also want to develop fast and have fun launching multiple games per year and not work multiple years per game. Our company has grown to fifteen people and two teams, we want to create a third team that fits our culture and can own part of our company. We have a very flat organization with full transparency and ownership, where the teams own their games. Our first two hit titles Farm Away! and Build Away! were both developed in less than half a year and both have won over the hearts of millions of players all around the world. You could be working on the next one…
