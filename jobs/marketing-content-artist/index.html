Marketing Content Artist

Back to all jobs

Want to be part of one of Finland’s hottest gaming startups? Is working with awesome mobile games your passion? How about creating and owning artwork which defines the identity of our games and brand across store pages, social media, and user acquisition campaigns? Please join Futureplay, we are looking for you!

We are looking for a motivated and passionate Marketing Content Artist to join us. You’ll have a portfolio of proven quality and be ready to apply your creative skills to the production of a variety of content for different media channels. You enjoy working as part of a team, but have a proactive approach with a hunger to take responsibility on an individual level.

What you will be doing:
  • Work closely with marketing and game teams to take initiative and ownership of marketing content creation.
  • Production of static, gif and video assets for social media and user acquisition channels.
  • Iterative production and testing of App Store assets including screenshots, icons and trailers.
  • Collaborate with game teams during production to develop a strong IP, consistent brand and clear vision.
  • Collaborate with game artists to composite existing 2D/3D assets in After Effects or equivalent.
What we require:
  • Exceptional skills in After Effects and Photoshop or equivalent video editing and compositing software.
  • An exceptional eye for composition, contrast, color, typography and design.
  • Good 2D animation and illustration skills.
  • Basic 3D tools knowledge.
  • Able to work with others to iterate and improve upon creatives in response to data.
  • Ability to follow existing art direction with your own vision and identity.
  • Strong communication skills - a willing and understanding listener who’s confident, assertive and open to feedback.
What we love, but don’t require:
  • Games industry experience.
  • A data-driven mindset.
  • Social media content marketing experience.
  • Experience with content for paid user acquisition campaigns.
  • Unity Engine experience.
  • Spine Animation tool knowledge.
What we offer:
  • Competitive compensation and perks.
  • Great working conditions in an exciting, agile environment .
  • Space to grow and learn.
  • True ownership and responsibility in a highly creative environment.
  • Flat hierarchy with a “no bullshit” culture.
  • Work in one of the most relevant game development capitals in Europe.
How to apply:

If you’re the talent we are looking for, send your application and CV in English together with a relevant portfolio to careers@futureplaygames.com with the subject “Marketing Content Artist”.

About Futureplay

Futureplay was founded in 2015 by five professionals to build the future standard for mobile games - and to create a great company where the best people want to work in. We want to develop fast and have fun launching multiple games per year and not work multiple years per game. Our company has grown to over 20 people with multiple teams, and we have a very flat organization with full transparency and ownership. Our first three titles Farm Away!, Build Away! and Craft Away! have won over the hearts of more than 20 million players worldwide and we have even more exciting projects in the pipeline. We’d love you to join us.
