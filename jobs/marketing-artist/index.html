Graphic Designer / Video Artist

Back to all jobs

We are looking for a skilled Graphic Designer/Video Artist to work in the Marketing Content Team at Futureplay! Working across multiple titles in a spectrum of art styles.

Want to work in the hottest game startup in Helsinki and ship multiple games a year? Is developing games your passion? Join Futureplay, we are looking for you!

Your Mission
  • Responsible for creating Static/Video Creatives from marketing assets.
  • Producing engaging social media creatives that endeavour to create impact, with a flair for humour.
  • Maintaining an organized production pipeline in order to quickly iterate on existing creatives.
  • Working to repurpose content in order to create scale and deliver across multiple platforms.
  • Realising video content from capture to final product, with input from internal stakeholders.
  • Close collaboration with the Marketing and Production teams.
  • Working with external partners to maintain brand styles.
Your Profile
  • Expert in Graphic Design.
  • Experience with Video Editing and a willingness to develop within this skill set.
  • Design skills, a good sense of composition with the ability to think outside the box.
  • Passionate about mobile games.
What we offer:
  • True ownership and responsibility in one of the most creative gaming startups
  • Competitive compensation and perks
  • Great working conditions in an exciting, agile environment
  • Space to grow and learn
  • Flat hierarchy with a “no bullshit” culture
  • Work in one of the most relevant game development capitals in Europe
How to apply:

If you’re the talent we are looking for, send your application and CV in English together with a relevant portfolio to careers@futureplaygames.com with the subject “Graphic Designer/Video Artist”.

About Futureplay

Futureplay was founded in early 2015 by a team of five Finnish gaming veterans who shared a vision to transform the way mobile games are played around the world. Based in Helsinki, we have since released six titles that have been played by over 50 million people worldwide and expanded our team to more than 25 people.

With a focus on creating beautifully designed, easy-to-pick-up-and-play mobile games, our current live portfolio includes Idle Farming Empire, Idle City Empire, Idle Crafting Empire, Idle Tuber Empire and Battlelands Royale.
