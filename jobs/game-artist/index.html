Senior Game Artist / Game Artist

Back to all jobs

Want to work in the hottest game startup in Helsinki and ship multiple games a year? Is developing games your passion? Join Futureplay, we are looking for you!

We are looking for an exceptionally skilled Game Artist(s) to join our growing, experienced team. You have great conceptual skills, and ability to efficiently narrow down and iterate towards the best possible design. Your vision and exceptional artistic skills will help to shape the future of mobile gaming.

Using your wide skill set and variety of tools, you will add style and color to our Environments, Characters, Interfaces and Marketing art. You always push for fun, high quality art that welcomes and draws people to our games. Besides beautiful aesthetics, your work always supports gameplay and never sacrifices usability.

We require:
  • Extremely creative and self sufficient artist with proven experience from gaming or a similar industry
  • Great illustrations skills and appreciation to user interface design
  • Ability to create beautiful characters and environments from concepts to final game and marketing assets
  • A strong understanding of the elements of design
  • Ambition to push for fun, high quality art that our players will love
  • Understanding of Animation principles
  • Genuine interest towards user experience and usability
We admire:
  • Great user interface design skills
  • Ability to work independently within Unity
  • Proven experience with a relevant 3d modeling or sculpting tools
  • Experienced with Spine or other animation software
  • Keen eye and experience in behavioral economics
  • Motion graphics and/or video editing experience
We offer:
  • True ownership and responsibility in one of the most creative gaming startups
  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Great working conditions in an exciting and agile environment
  • Flexible working hours
  • Space to grow and learn
  • Flat hierarchy with a “no bullshit” culture
  • Work in one of the most relevant game development capitals in Europe
How to apply:

If you’re the talent we are looking for, send your application and CV in English together with a relevant portfolio to careers@futureplay.fi with the subject “Game Artist”.

About Futureplay

Futureplay, was founded in 2015 by five professionals to build the future standard for mobile games - and to create a great company where the best people want to work in. Our focus is on creating a new category of “View-to-Play” games – broadly accessible gameplay, with a natural integration and rewarding ad-based monetization. We also want to develop fast and have fun launching multiple games per year and not work multiple years per game. Our company has grown to fifteen people and two teams, we want to create a third team that fits our culture and can own part of our company. We have a very flat organization with full transparency and ownership, where the teams own their games. Our first two hit titles Farm Away! and Build Away! were both developed in less than half a year and both have won over the hearts of millions of players all around the world. You could be working on the next one…
