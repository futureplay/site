About Futureplay Games




"We believe in fast execution"

We are passionate about games, great games. The landscape of mobile games is evolving faster than ever and there has never been a better time to be a developer, publisher or more importantly a gamer. We believe in fast execution; we want to launch multiple games per year and not spend multiple years per launch.

​

Futureplay Games was founded in 2015 by five guys who wanted to build a company they would all want to work in - and to be their passion, workplace, hobby and home away from home to develop and publish great games.

The idea to focus on the growing mobile video ad market was brewed over multiple years, companies and cups of coffee. When starting up something from scratch it felt smarter to do something different and not to copy what everyone else was already doing. The world is never ready, but keeps on evolving, change is just hard to understand when change is taking place, but it is easy to notice when change has happened and you can spot the difference.

Futureplay Games has been successful with its first two titles "Farm Away!" and "Build Away!" both launched in the first year of starting initial development. The company has attracted more talent and tripled their team size in the first year.
