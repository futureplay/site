Zero hierarchy

Complicated organization models and hierarchy kill creativity and leave no room for play. Futureplay is a streamlined, lean organization. No office politics, no big egos – no bullshit. When we say we’re a family, we mean it. And as a family, we need to protect ourselves from hellish corporate structures like middle management. Accordingly, our only rules are as follows: Give your best, share and care. Nothing else matters.

"A great warrior is also a scholar, and a poet, and an artist" -Steven Seagal

Everybody gets to play

Good things come to those who play. We’ll let you play as much as you want because we believe in you. At Futureplay, teams are completely autonomous and free to do their thing as top-notch game developers. You can crunch hours when you’re on a roll, and, I when the situation so dictates, you can leave early. Besides, it’s more fun that way.

Results over processes

We like to think ourselves as a goal-driven company. We play fast, fail fast, pick up the pieces and move on fast. That means no rigid processes, no endless iterations and no burnout epidemics. We keep our eye on shipping, and ship we will. But don’t take our word for it, check out our track record.
