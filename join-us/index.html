This is how we work




Create

We need and want people to be creative. Take risks, sometimes fail, have constraints, but never be boring or too predictable.

Own

We want our teams to own their work, their games and part of the company. With ownership comes responsibility, pride and great results.

Launch

We want to launch multiple times per year and not spend multiple years per launch. This way we all learn, adapt, grow and get more awesome faster.
